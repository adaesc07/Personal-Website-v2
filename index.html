Feline

They move like shadows, silent and sure, Golden eyes gleaming, wild and pure. Masters of mystery, wrapped in grace, Soft-footed hunters in moonlight’s embrace. A flick of the tail, a whisper, a sigh, Perched on high places, watching you try. Independent yet yearning, distant yet near, They purr in your lap, dissolving your fear. Night-born dancers, rulers of dreams, Carrying secrets in their silken seams. In a world too loud, too fast, too bright, A cat is a pause—soft, still, and right.
